About the BMW Performance Center

The BMW Performance Driving School is excited to provide the opportunity to drive a wide variety of vehicles on our closed track. During your event you will get to participate in three driving experiences where you will feel the vehicle dynamics, the power of the engine and the true performance of our BMWs. A professional BMW instructor will guide you through each exercise. You will drive the X vehicles on our Off Road Course going through our deep water crossing, climbing our extreme hills and navigating Spiral Mountain. Our Performance Track exercise will get your adrenaline flowing as you take corners and straight-a-ways in several BMWs. You will see side by side how they handle the road. And the program would not be complete without the thrill of a lifetime, a Hot Lap with one of our Pro instructors! Register today for one of our sessions; with so many options to choose from, we are sure you will find one to accommodate your schedule.

FAQs

How do I check-in for my Performance Drive experience?

Upon arriving Performance Center, all registered participants must visit the reception counter to check in. 

What kind of shoes should I wear?

Please wear closed-toed shoes.

Do I need insurance to drive?

No, insurance is not required.

Do I need a driver's license?

Yes, you must present a valid driver's license upon check-in.

Is there an age-limit to drive?

You must be 18 years old and must present a valid driver's license upon check-in.

What is the cost to drive?

There is no cost to attend the Performance Drive for registered conference attendees and is offered on a first come first serve basis.

Will I still drive if there is bad weather?

Every event we have scheduled is held rain or shine, so bring clothing appropriate for the day's activities.
