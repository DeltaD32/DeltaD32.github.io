OUR PURPOSE

Our mission is to actively seek out and partner with a diverse range of suppliers that reflect our customers.

We believe that engaging a wide range of suppliers is not just the right thing to do, but also a strategic imperative that drives innovation, reduces risk, and fosters economic growth for all. By prioritizing this approach, we commit to building strong, long-term relationships with our suppliers, promoting equity and inclusion in the broader business ecosystem, and creating sustainable value for our customers, shareholders, and society at large.
